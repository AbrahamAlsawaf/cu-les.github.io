The Volesean system of units

Volume

Vole
A measure of gin which appears just a little overzealous, plus a little bit more. Also the amount of gin which will evaporate in one fortnight (7.5 pub-quizzes) when placed 1 half-mile from a fire of one Duncan.

Gill
The amount of gin required to get Gill to fall repeatedly off a chair. Equal to 6 Voles.

Distance

Coomber
The distance sidestepped by Chris.

Half-mile
Half of one mile.

Time

Pub-quiz
The amount of time taken for one of Harry's quizzes.

Fortnight
7.5 pub-quizzes.

Mass

Imperial buggerload
The amount of wood required to build a fire of one Duncan that will burn for one fortnight.

Metric buggerload
A bit less.

Fire

Duncan
The amount of fire at which it is comfortable to sit at a distance of one half-mile.
